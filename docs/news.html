Full Schedule for the rest of 2024

NEXT MEETING: Wednesday, July 10, 2024 10 am to 1:30 pm
Severna Park Community Center, Boone Station Hall

BLANKET WORK DAY

Join volunteers in making blankets for children who are ill or in need. We’ll be getting fleece ready for Backpack Buddy blankets and for crocheted edges, and bundling our recent fabric donations into kits ready to go and sewing some of those kits. Come with questions, leave with fabric and/or yarn for your next project.

The large room and table space make it easy to assemble large blankets. Visit with friends and bring a sack lunch if you wish to stay.

Please bring a needle and thread to help sew labels on finished blankets and a rotary cutter to help make fleece ready to use. Cutting mats and other supplies are available. In addition, if you can, please bring irons, ironing boards or ironing mats, sewing machines and extension cords and let’s see how many more blankets we can turn out during the meeting.

Reminders

  • PLEASE MAKE SURE YOU’VE REMOVED ALL PINS before donating a blanket. PL National sent out a message that a child had been hurt by a pin left in a seam. When labeling blankets, please check carefully for any errant pins.
  • It’s never too soon to work on Backpack Buddy blankets. Last year between the schools and the Stanton Center we donated 380 double fleece blankets! The demand will likely be higher this year - so get sewing!
  • When donating yarn, PLEASE give it to the Chapter Coordinator and DO NOT put it directly into the yarn bins (We’ve been getting too many wool and wool blends, which we can’t use for Project Linus blankets)
  • At the recent retreat, it was announced that Project Linus has now donated more than 10,000,000 blankets!

REGISTER FOR THE KNIT AND CROCHET ALONG BY MARCH 22
Registration link available at https://www.projectlinus.org/knitcrochet/ beginning March 2nd. Downloadable pattern instructions will be sent to you on March 23rd 2024.

COST: $20 donation to Project Linus for either the crocheted or knitted pattern or you can sign up for both for $40. Please designate the ANNAPOLIS PROJECT LINUS CHAPTER on your registration as the recipient of your donation so that the donation is applied to our chapter.

If you miss the March 22nd registration cutoff, don't give up! You can still register and get the patterns! However, the cost increases to $25/$50 and you will not be able to direct your donation to our chapter (it will be shared among all PL chapters).
