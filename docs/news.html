News and Events

Next Meeting: March 16, 2024
Quilters ahoy! Join the Anne Arundel County Group of Project Linus at an AFTERNOON MEETING on Saturday, March 16, 2024, from 2:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m., at the Severna Park Community Center, MAGOTHY ROOM, 623 Baltimore-Annapolis Blvd., Severna Park, MD 21146.
We’ll be focused on our quilters for this meeting as we have received a large donation of beautiful quilting cotton. We invite the quilters to come and choose fabric for their next Project Linus quilts. We’ll have tables and cutting mats available to get your next quilts up and running!
We’ll also have a table of “Last Chance” fabrics that have been around for a while that will be passed onto another charity if they don’t go on Saturday. There will be no fleece at this meeting, but we will have the bags of donated yarn available for our knitters and crocheters.

Feb 14, 2024
Stop by the Severna Park Community Center, Boone Station Hall, 627 Baltimore Annapolis Blvd. Severna Park, MD 21146 Wednesday, FEBRUARY 21, 2024, from 10:00 am till approximately 2:00 pm and join volunteers in making blankets for children who are ill or in need. Come with questions, leave with fabric and/or yarn for your next project. The large room and table space make it easy to assemble large blankets. Visit with friends and bring a sack lunch if you wish to stay.
Please bring a needle and thread to help sew labels on finished blankets and a rotary cutter to help make fleece ready to use. Cutting mats and other supplies are available. In addition, if you can, please bring irons, ironing boards or ironing mats, sewing machines and extension cords and let’s see how many more blankets we can turn out during the meeting.
Feb 10, 2024
Join the Anne Arundel County Group of Project Linus at an AFTERNOON MEETING on Saturday, February 17, 2024, from 2:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m., at the Severna Park Community Center, MAGOTHY ROOM, 623 Baltimore-Annapolis Blvd., Severna Park, MD 21146. Volunteer Sandy Campbell will demonstrate how to braid the edges of double fleece blankets, which is a quick way of making those Backpack Buddy Blankets (October will creep up on us before we know it!). Pick up fabric or yarn for your next project, bring blankets that you’re especially proud of for the Show and Tell.
