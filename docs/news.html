News and Events

Feb 10, 2024
There's snow in the forecast! We'll post here if the meeting is cancelled due to weather, but our Facebook page will probably be updated sooner.
Join the Anne Arundel County Group of Project Linus at an AFTERNOON MEETING on Saturday, February 17, 2024, from 2:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m., at the Severna Park Community Center, MAGOTHY ROOM, 623 Baltimore-Annapolis Blvd., Severna Park, MD 21146. Volunteer Sandy Campbell will demonstrate how to braid the edges of double fleece blankets, which is a quick way of making those Backpack Buddy Blankets (October will creep up on us before we know it!). Pick up fabric or yarn for your next project, bring blankets that you’re especially proud of for the Show and Tell.

Severna Park Joann Fabrics Closing Soon
The Severna Park Joann Fabrics will be closing in the very near future. They're currently offering substantial discounts on remaining inventory.

The Severna Park store will no longer be a Project Linus drop-off location. Our other locations will continue to be active. These are the Annapolis Joann Fabrics store and the reception desk of the Severna Park Community Center.
